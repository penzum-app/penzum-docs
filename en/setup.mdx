After signing in for the first time, Penzum will ask for a few details needed for tax estimates and personalization.

## Step 1 — Tax form

Choose the tax form that applies to your business. If you're unsure, ask your accountant. See the [tax form guide](/en/tax-form).

## Step 2 — Business type

| Type | When to choose |
|---|---|
| Full-time | Your business is your sole source of income |
| Part-time | You also have an active employment contract |
| Retired | You run your business alongside a pension |

## Step 3 — Connect your invoicing software *(optional)*

Connect your Számlázz.hu or Billingo account to automatically sync invoices.
- [Connect Számlázz.hu →](/en/szamlazzhu)
- [Connect Billingo →](/en/billingo)

## Step 4 — Connect your bank *(Navigator)*

<Info>
  Bank connection is available on the **Navigator** plan only.
</Info>

Grant read-only access to your bank account for automatic transaction imports. See [Bank connection](/en/bank).
