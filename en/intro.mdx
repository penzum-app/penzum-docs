---
title: What is Penzum?
description: Financial and tax dashboard for Hungarian sole traders
---

Penzum is a financial management app built for Hungarian sole traders (egyéni vállalkozók). It helps you track income and expenses, monitor tax obligations, and make informed decisions — all in one place.

## What does it do?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Income" icon="file-invoice">
    Automatic sync with your invoicing software keeps your income up to date.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Expenses" icon="receipt">
    Categorized expense tracking, including automatic bank imports.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tax estimate" icon="calculator">
    Real-time tax burden estimate based on your income and tax form.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Obligations" icon="calendar">
    Deadline calendar for monthly and quarterly tax payments.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who is it for?

Penzum is designed for **flat-rate tax (átalányadó) and KATA sole traders** — whether full-time or part-time.

## What Penzum is not

<Warning>
  Penzum is not an accounting tool and does not replace your accountant. It does not submit filings to the Hungarian tax authority (NAV). The AI Advisor is for informational purposes only — always consult a professional before making tax decisions.
</Warning>
